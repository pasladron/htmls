StartupCircle is a 5000+ Entrepreneur organization dedicated to bringing together Innovators, Resources and Investors under a System of Trust .

StartupCircle relies on breakthrough studies, methods and strategies to create a collaborative and innovative environment to support tomorrow’s leaders, helping a new generation of entrepreneurs generate new sources of value, growth and competitive advantage.

The StartupCircle Acceleration program is being implemented by a select group of non-profit and profit incubators in Southern California — incubators launched by Cities, Economic Development Corporations, Small Business Development Centers, Colleges and Universities, Real Estate Developers, etc.

Alibre.io pitched among 17 other selected start-ups (from a pool of close to 200) winning the San Diego Startup Circle and earning a spot at the Plug and Play Tech Center 3 month accelerator program in Silicon Valley in the summer of 2014.
